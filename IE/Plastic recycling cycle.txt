The diagram illustrates the progress of recycling reusable plastic bottles. There are six main steps in the process, beginning with people buying and using and ending with reselling the recycled plastic products.

First, people buy and use the product from stores. After that, plastic products are classified into reusable and unusable, such as plastic bottles for reusable and plastic bags, rubbish for unusable. After classification, the reusable products are gathered for the next step.

Then, it is sent to sorting house to be sorted by type, color, and size. In the next stage, sorted plastic products is transported to the processing plant where process of recycling reusable plastic products is made. After being recycle, products are sent to store and begin a new cycle of plastic products.
